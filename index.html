iPhone 12

The iPhone 12 provides the best user experience, with the A14 Bionic chip and a Super Retina XDR OLED display, protected by a new Ceramic Shield. The iPhone 12 can take superb pictures even in Night Mode with its 12-megapixel dual camera system which includes a wide and an ultra-wide angle lens. All of these exceptional features are complemented by a fresh new design, a MagSafe system designed to charge in wireless mode and support for faster 5G cellular networks.
